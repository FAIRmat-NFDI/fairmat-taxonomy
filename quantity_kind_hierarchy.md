## Quantity Kinds Dimension Vector

* A: Amount of substance (mol)
* E: Electric current (A)
* L: Length (m)
* I: Luminous intensity (cd)
* M: Mass (kg)
* H: Temperature (K)
* T: Time (s)
* D: Dimensionless (1)
